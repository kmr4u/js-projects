#Rules

1. Click on RollDice to roll a dice which represents a random number from 1-6.
2. The current score represents the sum of your rolled dice numbers in the current round.
3. When you roll a 1, you loose all your current score and the controll passes to the other player.
4. If you dont wan't to roll, you can choose to hold.
5. When you choose to hold, your current score adds up to your total score and controll passes to the other player.
6. The player who first reaches the score of 100 wins the game.
